About me

I'm Andrew Morrison, a software engineer who is passionate about creating meaningful solutions. My focus on radical empathy allows me to combine technical expertise with a genuine understanding of others' needs. Throughout my journey, I have worked on a range of projects that showcase my abilities. For instance, I developed Snake, a classic arcade game, using JavaScript and CSS. Another notable project is VYNL, a social app that enables users to showcase their favorite records and explore their friends' unique musical tastes.

In addition to my software engineering expertise, I have gained valuable experience in various roles. As a Bartender at North Moore Oysters LLC, I excelled in sales and built strong relationships with customers. In my role as a Rock Department Lead at Camp Ballibay, I successfully managed projects and prioritized the success and well-being of my team. Previously, as a Music Instructor at Eugene's Music Studio, I crafted personalized curriculums for each student and established lasting connections with parents to ensure their children's growth.

I acquired a diverse skill set during my time in General Assembly's Software Engineering Intensive program. This education equipped me with proficiency in JavaScript, React, HTML5, CSS3, Git, and other relevant technologies. With my combination of technical expertise and empathetic approach, I am ready to contribute to impactful projects and make a difference in the world of software engineering.
